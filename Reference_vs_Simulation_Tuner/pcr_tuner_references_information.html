Influenza A Virus Strains

Background Information:
Lin et al. (2008) describe an HRM assay targeting nucleotides 238–416 of the influenza A M gene. While the original article does not provide complete nucleotide sequences for the reference strains, the amplicon sequences used in this simulator were reconstructed from publicly available GenBank M-gene references containing exact matches to the assay primers:

• A/California/07/2009 (H1N1) — NC_026431.1
• A/Wisconsin/67/2005 (H3N2) — EU100611.1
• A/Canada/rv504/2004 (H7N3) — CY015007.1 (synthetic control source)

These sequences were used exclusively to reconstruct the amplicon region (nucleotides 238–416) and are not the identical isolates used in the original HRM experiment.

Reference: https://journals.asm.org/doi/10.1128/jcm.02015-07
